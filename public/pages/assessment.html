Timed Practice Assessment

Please follow the instructions below carefully and accurately. This assessment will be timed, please complete it as quickly as you can while still being accurate. Accuracy is more important than speed.

  1. Create a Google Docs document named MyName01 where “MyName” is your first name. For example, if your name is John your file will be called John01.
  2. On the first line of the document, type the time you started this assessment.
  3. Find a resource online that teaches HTML. Paste a link to that resource into the next line of the document.
  4. Take this one minute typing test.
  5. Copy your results from the page, be sure to include your Typing Speed, Accuracy and Net Speed. Paste it into your Google Docs document.
  6. Share the document with apply@refcode.org. (Be sure to leave "Notify people" checked.)
  7. Download the document as a PDF.
  8. From your email account, email the document as an attachment to apply@refcode.org with the subject line "Refcode Assessment" and with the current time written in the body of the email.
