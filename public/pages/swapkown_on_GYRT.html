How to Swapkown with GYRT

You may have had a homework assignment that instructs you to use your keyboard to perform a swapkown on your GYRT. The purpose of this is to give you instructions for something that you don't know the answer to. Because there's no such thing as a swapkown and GYRT means nothing!

So how do I swapkown?

If you found this page, it's because you did a Google search to figure this out for yourself, so congratulations, you have succeeded in your swapkownery of GYRT. The other way you could have succeeded was to post a question in Slack to the effect of "What is a swapkown?" or "How do I swapkown a GYRT" or "what is GYRT?"

Yah, we got U!
