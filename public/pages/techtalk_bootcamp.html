Erin shaw

Erin Shaw is a software engineer at Mailchimp. She has been a programmer for almost five years. Before her career in tech, she was an archivist and corporate historian. With a B.A. and M.A. in American History, she decided to attend The Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy to lean full stack JavaScript in 2017. Afterwards, she was a participant at Recurse Center, a programming retreat located in New York City.
